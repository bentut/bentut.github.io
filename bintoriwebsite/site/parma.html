Castello di Tabiano

Parma, Italy was founded as a Roman colony in 183 BC. When Ben and Tori arrive in Parma, it will be the first time either of them has ever set foot there. Their marriage certificate will be both a part of a 2000 year history, and a symbol of a new adventure and unknown territory.

Beautiful, rustic Parma will serve as the final departure point for Ben and Tori’s journey as partners and the kickoff to the project of building a life together. Not sure what they will encounter along the way or what problems they will need to solve. But they know they will need a lot of assistance — from the people, places and traditions that got them here, and from those friends and family that can meet them along the way.

More than anything, they are excited to set off together! And are endlessly grateful for confidence, inspiration, love, and aloha they receive from the special places and people in their lives.

O kau aku, o ka ia la mai, pelā ka nohona o ka ‘ohana. ‘Ōlelo No‘eau, No. 2441

From you and from him - so lived the family. Or, the farmer gave to the fisherman, the fisherman gave to the farmer, sharing.

These are people whose work feeds others in a way that educates the community about kalo. The goal is to share their knowledge, connect, and eat great food with friends and family.
